## CROSS-CHANNEL NOTES

**Keep them separate.** Different audiences, different tones. Don't cross-promote between the two channels — it confuses the algorithm and the viewer.

**Batch record.** Film 4–6 videos in one session. Shorts can be cut from long-form outtakes.

**Thumbnails matter more than titles.** Both niches are visual — invest in clean, high-contrast thumbnails with one clear focal point.

**Best time to post:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9am–12pm in your target timezone.

**Tools to use:**
- TubeBuddy or VidIQ for keyword research
- CapCut or Descript for editing
- Canva for thumbnails
